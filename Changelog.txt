--- v1.0 (Jan 22, 2007)
	- Initial release under GPLv2. 
	- Supports 10 inputs (4 direction + 6 buttons), mapped as follows:	
	   PORTC5: Up
	   PORTC4: Down
	   PORTC3: Left
	   PORTC2: Right
	   PORTC1: Button 0
	   PORTC0: Button 1
	   PORTB5: Button 2
	   PORTB4: Button 3
	   PORTB3: Button 4
	   PORTB2: Button 5  (JP2 on multiuse PCB 2)
    - JP1 is still available for future expansion
